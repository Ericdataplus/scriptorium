Texts & sources

This is a personal reading site assembled from public-domain and openly licensed texts. Every text here can be obtained from its original source below.

Scripture

  • English Bible translations (KJV, WEB, ASV, YLT, Tyndale, Geneva, Douay-Rheims, JPS 1917, and others) — public domain, via Project Gutenberg and open Bible-text collections.
  • Greek New Testament — MorphGNT / SBLGNT.
  • Hebrew Bible (WLC) — OpenScriptures morphhb.
  • Latin Vulgate & other section-keyed texts — the Tesserae Project.
  • Brenton's English Septuagint — public domain, via bolls.life.

Classical & Hellenistic authors

  • Greek texts of Plato, Plutarch, Josephus, Herodotus, Homer & others — the Perseus Digital Library canonical texts (CC BY-SA 4.0; this site reuses those texts with thanks) and the Open Greek & Latin First1KGreek project (CC BY-SA).
  • English translations — public-domain versions from Project Gutenberg (Jowett's Plato, Whiston's Josephus, Macaulay's Herodotus, Dryden's Plutarch, Butler's & Pope's Homer, Evelyn-White's Hesiod, Yonge's Philo) and pre-1930 Loeb translations now in the US public domain (Fowler/Lamb/Bury's Plato, Perrin's Plutarch), as digitized by Perseus.
  • Ante-Nicene Fathers (Justin Martyr, Origen) — the 1885 Roberts–Donaldson translations, public domain, via CCEL and Early Christian Writings.

Annotations

The cross-references, typology atlas, gospel synopsis, and scholar verdicts are this site's own research notes, provided as-is for personal study.
