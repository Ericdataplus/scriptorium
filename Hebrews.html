About this reader

The Bible in up to twenty public-domain English translations beside the original (Greek · SBLGNT). Switch books from the top-left dropdown; Mark is the default. (Modern copyrighted versions are intentionally not included.)

Compare up to four translations side by side: tap the ℝ icon (or + version) to add a column, the translation menus to change each one, and × to drop one.

The cross-references are this project's own research findings, anchored to the verse and cited — a green bar marks verses where a connection was found. Each is tiered DEMONSTRABLE / strong-inference / speculative, and colour-coded:
